Our Story

Where East Meets West

Chi-Lates is a brand new fusion program — a one-of-a-kind class blending the best of Qigong, Tai Chi Flow, and Pilates, bringing ancient wisdom of Chinese medicine together with modern core-strengthening techniques.

Each class is designed to support your whole self — body, mind, and energy. You'll experience Pilates-inspired activation for deep core support and spinal enlivenment, Qigong principles for circulation and energy flow, and Tai Chi patterns that develop balance, coordination, and grounded power.

Chi-Lates strengthens gently without rigidity, tones and stabilizes while teaching the body how to move with fluidity and awareness. This class is open to all levels and is especially supportive for those who want to gently rebuild strength, seek stress and anxiety relief, and wish to enhance balance, spinal health, flexibility, and energy flow through mindful movement.

Each class concludes with a guided Tibetan sound bowl meditation for deep restoration and integration.

The Method

Three Traditions. One Practice.

Chi-Lates weaves together three powerful movement traditions into a seamless, flowing practice that enhances physical fitness, supports joint health, improves posture, and deepens mind-body connection.

Qigong

Deeply rooted in Chinese medicine, Qigong involves gentle movements and breathing practices designed for whole body health. By removing blockages and balancing the flow of energy within meridians, your body can do what it does best — heal itself naturally.

Tai Chi Flow

Tai Chi patterns develop balance, coordination, and grounded power. These slow, intentional walking flows bring a meditative quality that calms the nervous system and guides your internal Qi with beautiful movement.

Pilates

Pilates-inspired activation provides deep core support and spinal enlivenment. This precision work strengthens the deep stabilizing muscles of the body, building a strong foundation from the center out.

Your Guides

Meet the Teachers

All Chi-Lates classes are taught by two teachers, bringing together expertise from both Eastern and Western movement traditions.

Joellene Skoog

Certified Qigong & Tai Chi Flow Teacher • Meditation Coach

Joellene is a certified Qigong and Tai Chi Flow teacher with a deep passion for Chinese medicine and holistic wellness. She began her journey into Qigong and Tai Chi while pursuing her studies in Chinese medicine, motivated by her own healing experience after a life-changing car accident. Her journey has been guided by esteemed teachers, including Sifu Tai Chi Master David Fetyko and Sifu Qigong Master and Chinese Medicine Practitioner Lee Holden.

Anna Calloway

Certified Basa Pilates Instructor • Pilates Initiative

Anna brings her expertise as a certified Basa Pilates instructor to every Chi-Lates session. Through Pilates Initiative, she guides students toward deep core support and spinal enlivenment, creating a space where every body is welcome on their wellness journey.
